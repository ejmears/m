Elijah J. Mears

Writer, Composer, Philologist, Poet.

The UNCG Classical Society is a student organization dedicated to the promotion and appreciation of the Greco-Roman world. Elijah has been their webmaster and social media coordinator since August of 2015.
The Odyssey is a social discovery platform dedicated to amplifying millennial voices. Elijah has been a writer with the Wake County branch of Odyssey since April 2016.
The Coraddi is UNCG’s literary magazine. The poem Land of Setting Sun appeared in their Fall 2015 issue.
Elijah has been writing music and putting it on YouTube since 2012. He is also a member of Enlightened Productions, a networking group for young composers.
